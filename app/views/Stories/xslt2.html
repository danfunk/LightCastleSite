Dublin Core and Blacklight

The Scripps Library at the University of Virginia needed help standing up a new Blacklight instance and to transform a set of documents in various XML formats into Dublin Core, a common meta-data standard.

LightCastle provided a suite of XSLT transformations to convert from non-standard formats to Dublin Core. In addition, LightCastle assured that the XSLT's were easily viewable and adjustable in the Oxygen XML/XSLT editor so that the Scripps library could easily maintain these transformations on their own as new content arrived. Finally, LightCastle imported the final XML documents into Blacklight and tested the content in its final destination to assure that the Libraries would not encounter any surprises after the work was completed.
