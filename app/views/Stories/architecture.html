Detailed diagrams—as useful as they are attractive

While working at Rosetta Stone as a Senior Developer and Manager, Dan designed a number software architecture diagrams aimed at building consensus around Rosetta Stone’s growing web services and clarifying the roles of back-end developers. Featured before the highest levels of leadership and the entire company, Dan’s artifacts helped shape the implementation of major projects and helped build a common understanding of project goals.
