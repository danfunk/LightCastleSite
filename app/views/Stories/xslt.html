XML And XSLT

Silverchair brought in LightCastle initially to assist in a data migration project where data from an existing system needed to be transformed and ingested into the Silverchair publishing platform. The LightCastle team wrote an RoR and XSLT application to enable Silverchair to migrate many thousands of pages of academic journal content to the Silverchair platform. LightCastle then refactored Silverchair’s existing XSLT that had been developed in the past to migrate multiple prior customers. LightCastle modularized this code and built an XSLT library for Silverchair that could be used going forward and that would reduce the costs of both maintenance of existing customers and of implementation of new customers on the platform. LightCastle also made recommendations for improving data quality and for implementing better quality processes as the platform is further developed.
