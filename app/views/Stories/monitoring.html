Preventing Outages using Open Source Monitoring

In her own words: Tamara “monitors the heck out of every process or system [she] touches,” especially when any sort of failure occurs.

From server load to potential system outages and security breaches, Tamara Funk observes the entire matrix of systems to head off problems. And for her customers Funk interwove her meticulous monitoring with infrastructural stability—by insisting that critical servers be clustered in order to prevent major system breakdowns. With Nagios™ monitoring in place and custom plug-in scripts, stakeholders got exceptional visibility through her alerting system. Upgrade planning was made easier with Tamara's Cacti scripts and templates that produced graphs showing server statistics and impact of applications on the hardware.

#{sa_stories /}

Cacti is a complete network graphing solution designed to harness the power of RRDTool’s data storage and graphing functionality. Cacti provides a fast poller, advanced graph templating, multiple data acquisition methods, and user management features out of the box. All of this is wrapped in an intuitive, easy to use interface that makes sense for LANsized installations up to complex networks with hundreds of devices.
