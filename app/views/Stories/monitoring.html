Using Open Source Monitoring Application Cacti to Fine-tune Ntelos’ Servers

In her own words: Tamara “monitors the heck out of every process or system [she] touches,” especially when any sort of failure occurs.

From server load to potential system outages and security breaches, Tamara Funk observes the entire matrix of systems to head off problems. And for nTelos Wireless® Funk interwove her meticulous monitoring with infrastructural stability--by insisting that built servers entering mid-process be clustered in order to prevent a major system breakdown. With Nagios® monitoring in place and one of her characteristic plug-in scripts that she had customized, stakeholders got more than adequate visibility through her alert system.

Cacti is a complete network graphing solution designed to harness the power of RRDTool’s data storage and graphing functionality. Cacti provides a fast poller, advanced graph templating, multiple data acquisition methods, and user management features out of the box. All of this is wrapped in an intuitive, easy to use interface that makes sense for LANsized installations up to complex networks with hundreds of devices.
