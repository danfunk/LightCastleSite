Methods of Procedure

Tamara Funk’s work vastly improved the nTelos data center’s best practices as she tackled the direst of situations: a data center in need of electrical work and requiring a series of power-downs proved a less-than-desirable user experience for internal clients. Funk’s methods of procedure (MOP’s) were generated through close communication with her clients to truly understand their perspectives.
