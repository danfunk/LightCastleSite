Detailed, Step by Step Documentation

Tamara Funk’s work vastly improved a data center’s best practices as she and her teammates tacked a data center in need of electrical work. A series of power-downs were required for the entire data center to upgrade the electrical system. Having anything go wrong with the systems as they were powered off and brought back up would present a less-than-desirable user experience for internal clients. Tamara’s methods of procedure (MOP’s) were generated through close communication with her customers and teammates to make sure that nothing was missed. Linux/Unix server power-downs quickly evolved toward an error-free experience due to Tamara’s standardizations.
